Hi.

My name is Vlad. I am a rendering engineer from Estonia. I started my career path as a game developer in Unity, then Unreal Engine, and now I work on a proprietary game engine under NDA while also building [my own one](https://github.com/JoymanGD/Waldem).

I have broad experience across the game development pipeline, from game design, 3D modeling, and sound design to technical research, market research, analytics, and more. Rendering is still the area where I go deepest.

Besides that, I am interested in a few other directions too.
